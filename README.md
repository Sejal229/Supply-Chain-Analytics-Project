# Supply Chain Performance & Operational Efficiency Analysis 📊

An end-to-end data analytics project focusing on evaluating supply chain efficiency, identifying logistics bottlenecks, and optimizing inventory management using **Python**, **SQL**, and **Power BI**.

---

## 📌 Project Overview
This project provides actionable insights to improve customer satisfaction and reduce operational losses by analyzing logistics and inventory data. We transition from data preparation in Python to deep-dive strategic querying in SQL, culminating in an executive-level Power BI dashboard.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
* **Data Cleaning & Transformation:** Python (Pandas, NumPy)
* **Database Management & Analysis:** SQL (MySQL/PostgreSQL)
* **Data Visualization:** Power BI (DAX, Power Query)
* **Analytical Concepts:** Correlation Analysis, Outlier Detection, Inventory Gap Analysis.

---

## 📊 Key Business Problems Solved
* **Logistics Bottlenecks:** Identified locations where delivery delays directly impacted customer ratings.
* **Inventory Gaps:** Analyzed stock-to-demand ratios to highlight warehouses at high risk of stockouts.
* **Profitability Leaks:** Evaluated the financial impact of 'Fast Shipping' vs. 'Standard Shipping' costs.
* **Operational Efficiency:** Pinpointed underutilized warehouse spaces and damaged goods trends.

---

## 🚀 Project Workflow

### **Phase 1: Data Preparation (Python)**
* Cleaned and pre-processed the raw supply chain dataset.
* **Feature Engineering:** Created metrics like `Total_Logistics_Cost`, `Stock_to_Demand_Ratio`, `Capacity_Utilization`, and `Net_Profit`.
* Handled missing values and ensured data consistency for SQL integration.

### **Phase 2: Strategic Analysis (SQL)**
Executed complex queries to solve specific business questions:
* **Correlation Check:** Verified the relationship between shipping buckets and customer satisfaction.
* **Supplier Performance:** Ranked suppliers based on damage rates and processing times.
* **Financial Audit:** Identified high-loss cities by comparing logistics costs against profit margins.

### **Phase 3: Executive Dashboard (Power BI)**
Created an interactive 2-page dashboard:
1.  **Executive Insights:** High-level KPIs showing Net Profit, Avg Rating, and Total Costs.
2.  **Operational Efficiency:** A drill-down view into warehouse-specific risks, inventory gaps, and shipping impact scatter plots.
3.  **Action Plan:** A dynamic table providing recommended actions (**Replenish/Transfer/No Action**) based on real-time data.

---

## 📈 Key Insights
* **The "3.5 Day" Rule:** Customer ratings drop significantly once the average shipping time exceeds 3.5 days.
* **Inventory Crisis:** Over **55% of warehouses** were found to be understocked, leading to a massive inventory gap in key locations like Seattle and Denver.
* **The Logistics Paradox:** Fast shipping (0-3 days) showed higher operational losses compared to standard shipping, suggesting a need for route optimization.
